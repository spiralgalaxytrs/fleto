What is Fleto?

Fleto is a three-tiered service model that helps to maintain a productive fleet, enhance service, and increase revenue while concurrently reducing expenditures.An admin panel, which manages the whole system, as well as a taxi user app, which enables customers to book taxis, and a driver app, which allows drivers to accept bookings made through the app.

Why choose Fleto?
Fleto provides a customizable booking and administration system that is easy to use. It also incorporates payment gateways, marketing tools, dynamic pricing, detailed analytics, and other advanced features into an unified platform.
What makes Fleto a good taxi dispatch software?

Fleto is a cloud-based system and that easy to use. To be more competitive and grow your business digitally, Fleto provides advanced features like accurate GPS location to maximize rides in a given area and it can also accepts all forms of payments.

Is it possible to customize the software?

Fleto is entirely customisable to whatever extent necessary to meet your specific business requirements. We provide solutions to all of your challenges and are well-suited for both small and big fleets.

What are the payment options provided in Fleto?

All payment methods are supported by Fleto, including credit cards, debit cards, UPI (universal payment interface), wallets, and net banking, which helps to streamline, standardize, and enhance the payment process.
